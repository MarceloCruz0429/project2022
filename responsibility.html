Our Responsibility

We Raise With Care


We at Mermar Nature Farm,We work closely with our farmers and landowners to meet rigorously high standards of animal welfare, through excellent sanitation, nutrition and housing practices which support the health and well being of our pigs.


Sustainability is a key focus in everything we do at MerMar Nature Farm and we have committed to a rigorous set of standards which will make us one of the most sustainable farming businesses in the town. We have pledged to reduce CO2 emissions, monitor our water usage and our responsible use of antibiotics. Through reducing our environmental impact, we believe we can have a sustainable business plan set for the future.
